ABOUT ME

From the moment I first programmed my ZX Spectrum as a child, I have been fascinated by the possibilities of technology. From building radio-controlled aircraft to crafting FM radio transmitters (If I was not taking my mums hoover apart!). All while continuing my obsession with software development and programming languages.
Throughout my professional career, I have honed my critical thinking skills and cultivated a deep understanding of both hardware and software systems. My experience in electrical and mechanical engineering provided a solid foundation for logical thinking and problem solving, which seamlessly transitioned into programming and application development.
